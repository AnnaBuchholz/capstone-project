John Horton Conway

Life

John Conway was born on December 26, 1937, and he died on April 11, 2020, at the age of 82 from COVID-19 complications.

Mathematical Contributions

His most well-known contribution to mathematics was the creation of the game of life. And no, I don't mean the Game of Life board game. I mean a mathematical game where some initial input (of which "cells" are "living" or "dead") completely determines how the "game" is affected later. He also did much work on the Monster Group. So... this is reeeeaaaly complicated. Suffice it to say that the smallest dimesnsion it can be in is 196,883 and that it has about 10^54 elements in it.
